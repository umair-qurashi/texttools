Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024

Your Privacy Matters

At Text Tools, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our website and tools.

Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your text data. All text processing happens entirely in your browser using JavaScript. Your text never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What We Don't Collect

  • Text Content: We never see, store, or transmit the text you enter into our tools.
  • Personal Information: We don't require registration or personal information to use our tools.
  • Usage Data: We don't track your usage patterns or collect analytics about how you use our tools.
  • Cookies: We only use essential cookies (like theme preference stored in localStorage) that don't contain personal information.

Local Storage

We use browser localStorage to save your theme preference (dark/light mode) for a better user experience. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server. You can clear this data at any time through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Text Tools does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We don't integrate with external APIs that could collect your data. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Browser Information

Like most websites, our web server may automatically collect standard browser information such as IP address, browser type, and access times. This information is used only for basic server maintenance and security purposes and is not linked to any personal data or text content you enter.

Contact Forms

If you contact us through our contact form, the information you provide (name, email, message) is sent via email using a mailto link. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored on our servers or shared with third parties.

Children's Privacy

Text Tools is safe for users of all ages. We don't knowingly collect any information from children, and since we don't collect personal data, children can use our tools safely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no data to access, modify, or delete. However, you can:

  • Clear your browser's localStorage to remove theme preferences
  • Use our tools in incognito/private browsing mode for additional privacy
  • Disable JavaScript if you prefer (though this will prevent the tools from functioning)

Security

While we don't collect or store your data, we take security seriously. Our website uses standard HTTPS encryption to protect data in transit. However, since all processing happens locally in your browser, your text content is never transmitted over the internet.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us. We're happy to address any privacy-related questions you may have.

Consent

By using Text Tools, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please discontinue use of our website.
