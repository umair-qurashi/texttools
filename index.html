Frequently Asked Questions

What is a word counter?

A word counter is an online tool that analyzes your text and provides statistics such as word count, character count (with and without spaces), sentence count, paragraph count, and estimated reading time. It's useful for writers, students, and professionals who need to meet specific word limits or track their writing progress.

How does this word counter calculate words and characters?

Our word counter uses JavaScript to analyze your text in real-time. Words are counted by splitting the text on whitespace, characters are counted including all letters, numbers, and symbols, and sentences are identified by punctuation marks. The tool updates automatically as you type.

Is my text saved or uploaded anywhere?

No, your text is never saved, uploaded, or transmitted to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your privacy is our priority, and we don't collect or store any of your data.

How accurate is the reading time?

The reading time is an estimate based on an average reading speed of 200 words per minute. Actual reading time may vary depending on the complexity of the text and the reader's speed. It's provided as a helpful guideline rather than an exact measurement.

Can I use these tools on mobile devices?

Yes! All our tools are fully responsive and work seamlessly on mobile phones, tablets, and desktop computers. The interface adapts to your screen size for the best user experience.

Are these tools free to use?

Yes, all our text tools are completely free to use. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations. You can use them as often as you need without any restrictions.
