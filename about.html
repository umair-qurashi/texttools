About Us

Welcome to Text Tools

Text Tools is a collection of free, easy-to-use online utilities designed to simplify your writing and text formatting tasks. Our mission is to provide reliable, fast, and privacy-focused tools that help writers, students, professionals, and content creators work more efficiently.

Our Mission

We believe that everyone should have access to powerful text analysis and formatting tools without the hassle of complex software installations or privacy concerns. All our tools are:

  • Free to Use: No subscriptions, no hidden fees, no limitations
  • Privacy-Focused: All processing happens locally in your browser - we never collect or store your data
  • Fast & Reliable: Instant results with no server delays
  • Easy to Use: Clean, intuitive interfaces that work on any device
  • Always Available: Access your tools from anywhere, anytime

What We Offer

Currently, Text Tools offers a comprehensive Word Counter that provides real-time statistics including:

  • Word count
  • Character count (with and without spaces)
  • Sentence count
  • Paragraph count
  • Estimated reading time

We're continuously working to add more useful text tools to help you with various writing and formatting needs. Our goal is to become your one-stop destination for all text-related utilities.

Why Choose Text Tools?

In a world where privacy is increasingly important, Text Tools stands out by processing everything locally in your browser. Your text never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security. Whether you're working on a sensitive document, a school assignment, or creative writing, you can trust that your content remains private.

Our Commitment

We are committed to:

  • Maintaining the highest standards of user privacy
  • Providing accurate and reliable tools
  • Continuously improving our services based on user feedback
  • Keeping all tools free and accessible to everyone
  • Ensuring our tools work seamlessly across all devices and browsers
